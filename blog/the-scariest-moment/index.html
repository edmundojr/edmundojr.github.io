The Scariest Moment

01 May 2015

“The scariest moment is always just before you start. After that, things can only get better.”

— Stephen King

On each and every one of the blogs that I’d started in the past, I posted this quote. Why this quote, Galadriel? Perhaps it is because I am afraid, and it gives me courage.

Written by Edmundo Santos who lives and works in Cork, Ireland.
